This page explains how users of the **Profixa** app can delete their account and associated personal data.

---

## How to delete your account

Users can delete their account directly within the Profixa app by following these steps:

1. Open the **Profixa** app  
2. Go to **Settings**  
3. Select **Delete Account**  
4. Confirm the deletion request  

---

## What data is deleted

When an account deletion is requested, the following data is removed:

- User account information  
- Associated application data  
- Device-related identifiers linked to the account (where applicable)  

---

## Data retention

All user data is permanently deleted from our systems within **30 days** of the deletion request, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

---

## Need help?

If you are unable to delete your account within the app or need further assistance, please contact our support team through the app or via our official support channels.
